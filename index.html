Eaindra's recreated recipes

Most of my cooking during my college days was done purely by instinct. Whenever I asked my mother for precise measurements, she never gave me a straight answer, so I ended up learning how to gauge things intuitively as I learned how to cook. As a result, some of my recipes don't come with any measurements at all. It's all about going with the flow and trusting my culinary instincts!
