Vegetarian Dumplings

Description

Vegetarian dumplings for one of our friends! She doesn't eat meat, so has to make do with vegetable for the fillings. The original recipes can be found here, on this website, with steps on how to make both the wrap and the fillings. But I just buy the wrapping and make the fillings. Easier and less effort!

Pretty sure I didn't follow the recipe to a T, but here's the list of ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste
  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrot shredded (100g, maybe?)
  • Mushrooms (200g)
  • Half a stick of leek (60g)
  • Cabbage (200g)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • Salt and pepper for taste

Steps

  1. Chop all the veggies and fried them in the order of mushrooms, onions, and carrots for 2-3 minutes until lightly browned.
  2. Then add leek and cabbage with a little salt and cook until veggies are soft and cook through.
  3. Stir occasionally, and add a little water if needed.
  4. Add the garlic, ginger past and seasonings and cook for further minute to combine the flavors.
  5. Set aside to cool; and after cooling down, wrap it in your wrappings, cook it with steamer or pan-fried it... and viola! You get your vegetarian dumplings!
