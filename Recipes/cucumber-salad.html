Cucumber Salad

Description

A refreshing and spicy dish to pair with something oily or just a bowl of fried rice! I came upon this dish while I was craving cucumber but don't want to just eat it raw as it is. Original recipe is from Carlo Cao With limited ingredients at my disposal, I had to get creative. So, the following ingredients and measurements are what I ended up using.

Ingredients

  • Half a cucumber (the usual one you can get at Jumbo or Lidl)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon light soy sauce
  • Some sugar
  • Some water

Steps

  1. Place a cucumber between two chopsticks.
  2. Cut straight slices from top to bottom, the chopsticks create a barrier to prevent you from cutting through.
  3. Then flip the cucumber so that the uncut side faces up and cut them in 45 degrees, placing them in between chopsticks as well.
  4. For the sauce, mix all the ingredients (including sesame seeds) in a bowl... For the amount of water, I just go with how much the sauce thins out. I don't want it to be too thin, but at the same time not too thick that I cannot pour it over the cucumber.
  5. Pour the sauce over cucumbers, and leave it in the fridge for 30 minutes. The longer the better.
  6. You can serve it with anything really, if you like cucumber.
