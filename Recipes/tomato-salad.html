Tomato Salad

Description

A perfect dish to eat during hot summer. It's tangy, savory, and above all reminds me of my home.

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Onion (red or yellow)
  • Crushed peanut
  • Chili
  • Fried shallots (optional)
  • Gram flour
  • Fish sauce

Steps

  1. Cut tomato and onion.
  2. Pan fry gram flour on a dry frying pan. It is for fragrance
  3. Add crushed peanut.
  4. Add chili.
  5. Add fish sauce.
  6. Mix them up with fried shallots. (Optional)
  7. ... and serve!
